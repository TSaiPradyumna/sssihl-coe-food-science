SSSIHL Center of Excellence (CoE) in Food Processing & Preservation
The Official Repository for the SSSIHL Food Innovation Hub

Executive Summary
This repository houses the digital infrastructure for the Center of Excellence (CoE) in Food Processing and Preservation at the Sri Sathya Sai Institute of Higher Learning (SSSIHL).

The CoE serves as a translational hub, bridging advanced laboratory research with industrial application. Our mandate is to transform nutritional science into scalable, sustainable food technology, supporting everything from millet-based innovation to rigorous food safety validation. This digital platform is designed to provide transparency into our technical infrastructure, research capabilities, and service offerings for industrial and academic stakeholders.

Core Pillars
Research & Innovation: Advancing knowledge in Cereal/Millet technology, Dairy science, and shelf-life stability.

Infrastructure: Providing industry-grade access to pilot-scale processing equipment and analytical labs (including LC-Q-TOF-MS and Raman Spectroscopy).

Translational Support: Assisting startups and food processors with regulatory compliance (FSSAI/HACCP), product formulation, and commercialization.

Knowledge Transfer: A repository for industry-relevant training, certifications, and collaborative research initiatives.

Technical Architecture
This platform is architected for scalability, security, and high performance. It serves as the primary interface for:

Laboratory Booking & Scheduling: Streamlining industry and student access to high-end processing equipment.

Data Visualization: Presenting research outcomes and impact metrics for ongoing food safety projects.

Collaboration Portal: A gateway for FPOs, industry partners, and government agencies to connect with SSSIHL’s technical expertise.
